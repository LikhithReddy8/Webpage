PUBG

PUBG: New State has been announced as the newest game from PUBG Studio,
the makers of PLAYERUNKNOWN’S BATTLEGROUNDS. Krafton Inc. has announced the launch of the new battle royale game via a teaser video
on YouTube that shows off the game’s graphics, story, and gameplay. Much like the original PUBG game, PUBG: New State will see
“100 survivors face off until only one player or team remains.”
PUBG: New State pre-registration is now live on the Google Play Store. Unfortunately,
the game is not available in India due to the PUBG Mobile ban.
